🔩 Dental Implants

Dental implants are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth. A titanium post is surgically placed in the jawbone, creating a permanent foundation for a custom crown that looks, feels, and functions exactly like a natural tooth.

Benefits:
  • Permanent, lifelong solution
  • Preserves jawbone and facial structure
  • Natural look and feel
  • No adhesives or special cleaning needed

😁 Braces & Clear Aligners
