Got Questions?

Find answers to the most common questions about our dental treatments and services.

📋 General

What are MH Dental World's clinic hours?+

Monday to Saturday: 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM. Sunday: 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM (Prior Appointment Only). For emergencies, call +91 98456 61301 anytime.

Do I need to make an appointment?+

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend calling or WhatsApp-ing us in advance to minimize wait times and ensure your preferred specialist is available.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, UPI, Google Pay, PhonePe, and bank transfers. EMI options are available for select treatments.

Do you treat children?+

Absolutely! We have pediatric dental specialists who create a fun, gentle environment for children. We treat patients of all ages, from toddlers to seniors.

🦷 Treatments

Is dental treatment painful?+

We use advanced anesthesia, numbing gels, and gentle techniques to ensure virtually painless treatment. Most patients are pleasantly surprised by how comfortable their procedures are.

How long do dental implants last?+

With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last a lifetime. We use premium titanium implant systems from globally trusted brands.

How long does braces treatment take?+

Treatment duration varies from 6 months to 2 years depending on the complexity of alignment needed. Our orthodontist will provide a precise timeline after examination.

Is teeth whitening safe?+

Yes, professional teeth whitening performed by our dentists is completely safe. We use clinically tested products that are gentle on enamel while delivering dramatic results.

How often should I get a dental checkup?+

We recommend dental checkups every 6 months for most patients. Regular visits help catch problems early and maintain optimal oral health.

💰 Costs & Insurance

Do you offer EMI or installment payments?+

Yes! We offer 0% and low-interest EMI options for major treatments like implants, braces, and smile makeovers. Contact us for details on available plans.

Is there a consultation fee?+

Please call or WhatsApp us to know about current consultation charges and any ongoing offers. We're happy to assist.
